Bridged Messaging
-----------------

Objective-C:
    ``[aMutableArray addObject:@"someObject"]``

- Separate the selector:from the:arguments
- Smash_the_colons_
- Ditch.the_brackets_(and, add, arguments)

Python:
    ``aMutableArray.addObject_(u'someObject')``
